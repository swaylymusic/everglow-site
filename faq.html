FAQ

Questions clients usually ask before booking.

How long does a brow appointment take?

Most brow appointments take about 2 to 2.5 hours, including consultation, design, numbing, and the procedure.

Will the brows turn red or blue after 1–2 years?

Modern semi-permanent techniques and pigments are designed to fade more softly over time. Older permanent tattoo-style methods can leave strong red, blue, or dark residue, and some places may still use older approaches. Everglow’s positioning should emphasize soft healed results and natural fading.

Can you help with uneven brows?

Yes. Brow design should consider natural asymmetry, bone structure, eye shape, muscle movement, and existing brow hair before finalizing the shape.
